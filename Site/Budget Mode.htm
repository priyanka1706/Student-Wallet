So What is BUDGET MODE About?




Lets admit it; Budgets can be tough. So we've come up with a way to solve your woes - a fixed budget.
Budget mode takes in a preset budget, designed by you, for you. We'll take in your preferred target expense for the upcoming month, and we'll even reward you if you achieve them! But even if you don't, its alright. We'll help you set an easier target for you to achieve.
We've got your back, really.
